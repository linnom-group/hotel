What To Do
& Area Attractions

Nana pools national park is one of the most well-known attractions in the area. It is a wildlife conservation site declared as a UNESCO world heritage site in 1984. It is home to the biggest concentration of crocodiles and hippopotami in the country. It is also home to other endangered species like cape wild dog, cheetah, and lion.

Another interesting site to visit in the area is Matusadona National Park. It was declared a non-hunting site in 1958, a game reserve in 1963, and a national park in 1975. It has three different ecological areas, including Lake Kariba, Julbernardia and Brachystegia Woodlands Escarpment Area, and Zambezi Valley Floor.

You can also find Lake Kariba fascinating. Tourists should also visit Chinhoyi Caves. It is a group of dolomite and limestone caves declared as a national park in 1955. One of its most prominent features is the main cave, which has a pool of cobalt blue water. Lake Safari Lodge offers state-of-the-art facilities suitable for all kinds of functions. Caribbea Bay Resort provides rich accommodation experiences with affordability and practicality in mind.

The hotel is just around 1 mile away from the city center, and features 2 swimming pools.
